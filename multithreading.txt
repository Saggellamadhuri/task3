Document on Multithreading:
     Threading is a light weight process.Threading can be used to solve complicated prblms in background.
Threads can share the memory.
we have diff methods in threading:
1.run--->start point of a thread
2.start--->method starts by calling run method.
3.join([time])----->to terminate
4.is alive()------->checks whether it is present.
5.getname()--------->returns the name.
if no threads are present it will be taken as mainthread.
